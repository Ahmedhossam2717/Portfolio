Case study · Selected capability

The Daily Performance System

Problem

Cost and schedule deviations surfaced only at the monthly close — by then, corrective action was expensive and margin was already eroding.

Approach

Built a governed star-schema model in Power BI consolidating BOQ, budget, commitments, actual cost (SAP/Oracle), progress and manpower, with a live EVM engine in DAX.

Result

Cost and progress deviations now surface within 24 hours, with Board, Discipline and Cost-code views framed around decisions, not raw data.
